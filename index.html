CTU-Danao Unveils RFID System in Grand Ribbon-Cutting Ceremony

Few minutes ago

Cebu Technological University-Danao Campus celebrated the launch of its cutting-edge Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) system in a grand ribbon-cutting ceremony led by Campus Director Dr. Rose Mary L. Almacen. The event, graced by Danao City Mayor Hon. Thomas Durano and Globe Telecom Senior Manager Miss Ginalyn Villaceran, marked a significant leap towards modernizing campus operations.

Dr. Almacen highlighted the transformative impact of the RFID technology, stating, “This system will streamline processes and enhance campus safety for students and staff.” The initiative symbolizes CTU-Danao’s commitment to fostering a smarter and safer learning environment.
